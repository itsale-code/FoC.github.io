BMI Calculator:

This is a BMI calculator, you will input your weight and height and it will calculate your BMI (Body Mass Index) which determines if you are either: underweight, healthy, overweight or obese.


BMI RANGES:

  • Underweight is considered to be below (18.5)
  • Healthy is considered to be between (18.5–24.9)
  • Overweight is considered to be between 25.0–29.9
  • Obese is considered to be between ( >= 30.0)
